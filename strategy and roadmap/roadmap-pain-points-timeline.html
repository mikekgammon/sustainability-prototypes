The headline for Otto
Every customer pain raised on 7 May reaches full relief by Sep '26 — inside the first half of the FY27 Sustainability band. Pulling Ingestion redesign forward removes the long tail; the roadmap doesn't just cover the pains, it relieves all of them in one quarter.
1
Foundational modelling does the heavy lifting first
Three pains (calc accuracy, scope coverage, data trust) start lifting in Jul '26 the moment Foundational ships. Two of them turn fully green that month. This is why it's sequenced first — fastest broad relief per shipped workstream.
2
Strategy V2 unlocks the next wave
Three more pains (data trust, decision support, live tracking) move to full green when Strategy V2 ships in Sep '26. Combined with Ingestion redesign landing the same month, all seven pains are green by end of September.
3
Ingestion pulled forward — slow burner becomes a fast win
Under the original sequencing, manual ingestion would have been red for six months and partial-relieved only in Aug. Pulling Ingestion redesign forward (Paul's team already has a prototype landing in Q2 / July) closes that gap: full relief now lands in Sep alongside Strategy V2. The compounding effect — complex portfolio uploads becoming viable earlier — unblocks the scenario modelling and strategy comparison work downstream.

Assumptions in this view

Workstream delivery windows are illustrative — based on the FY27 roadmap Sustainability band (May–Nov '26) and the rough sequencing in Asana. Actual sprint dates will shift these. Relief is shown as landing at the end of each delivery window; in practice, value may ship incrementally during the window. Adjust window positions to match the live plan and the bars will redraw cleanly.
